Controls:
*Player1
	WS to move
	Mouse to move
	Space to fire
	E to spike the ball right after hitting it.

*Player2
	IK to move
	Mouse to move
	Enter to fire

*All players
	Num7 to turn Mouse controls off
	Num8 to turn Mouse controls on
	Num4 to turn two players controls off
	Num5 to turn two players controls on
	
Instructions:
	On the menu press play to start the game.
	Dont lose?
	Use the bullets to stun your opponent and then spike him.
	Careful not to get to close to each other. It gets pretty hard.
